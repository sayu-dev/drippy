Frequently asked questions 🧐

What is Drippy used for

It is a platform that helps you design your own clothes. You can choose from different components and variations to create a unique look, just like building LEGO. You can also share your designs with others, download patterns and assets, or request a physical sample.

Who would want to use Drippy

1. People who want to design their own clothes without any experience or skill required.
2. Fashion designers who want an easy way to design clothing without having to sketch or build anything from scratch
3. People who want to share their fashion designs with the world and get feedback from others
4. Fashion entrepreneurs who want to get started with their first product.

Is Drippy free to use?

It’s free and will forever be free! We also offer paid pricing plans to unlock premium features, templates, components, and assets to do even more with your creativity.

Can I share/download designs on Drippy?

You can now share your design on social media or share the link to view prototypes with others at the beta stage. However, the download feature is currently not available, but after the beta stage, you will be able to:
1. Download the sewing pattern physical production
2. Download all the assets of your design, like prints, graphics, or any images
3. Download the 3D design as FBX, OBJ, and multiple formats
4. Download the design as an image in different views.

Is Drippy available for use now?

We are in the private beta stage, meaning you would need to have our private invitation link to join our creator community. We want to ensure a seamless experience and that the platform is fully developed before the official launch. So if you want to be one of the early-adopters, click the “join private beta” button to join now..
